Autonomous Vehicles

The Self Driving Car

Self driving cars are vehicles that are piloted by a computer rather than a human driver.Even though the technology hasn't even been available to the general public yet, people have voiced concerns about the safety and potential risks of making this technology widespread.

Click to Scroll

Concerns

Without a human driver, people worry that an AI won't be able to have the same human-like reflexes and be able to react accordingly to high stress situations. Similarly an ethical question pops up when the topic of what a self-driving vehicle should do in the case of saving a pedestrian or risking the life of the passenger and several other qualities that a human might be able to make, but in the hands of a computer, many people would criticize its judgement. At an economic standpoint, the existence of self-driving cars could serve to completely derail the taxi/ride-share market.

Benefits

The benefits of self-driving cars outweigh the risks. Removing human error, such as tiredness, or being under the influence from the equation, make self-driving cars much safer, especially if a majority of cars on the road are self-driving.
